Our Mission

AMA Foundation is a nonprofit organization focused on helping young athletes grow through basketball. We provide high-level training, mentorship, and competitive opportunities to develop their skills. Our mission is to build strong, disciplined, and confident players on and off the court.

Our Core Values

Teamwork

Fostering collaboration and mutual respect among players.

Discipline

Instilling focus, dedication, and perseverance on and off the court.

Leadership

Developing confident individuals who inspire and guide others.

Community

Building a supportive network for athletes and families.
